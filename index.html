About the Convention

The SA Convention 2026 India brings together members, spouses, and friends in recovery from across the country and beyond for three days of fellowship, workshops, sharing, and service — hosted at the POC Center, KCBC Secretariat in Kochi. Financial aid is available for those who need support to attend, and international participants are warmly welcome.
